Who We Are

Company Overview

Sinar Mas Agribusiness and Food is a leading global agribusiness company operating across the entire palm oil value chain. As part of the Sinar Mas Group through Golden Agri-Resources Ltd (GAR), we have been at the forefront of sustainable agricultural practices since 1938.

With operations spanning cultivation, processing, and distribution, we are committed to delivering high-quality products while maintaining environmental stewardship and social responsibility. Our integrated operations ensure traceability and sustainability from plantation to end consumer.

Commitment

Sustainability Initiatives

Sustainability is at the core of our operations. We are committed to responsible palm oil production that protects the environment, respects human rights, and supports local communities.

  • No Deforestation: Zero deforestation policy protecting high conservation value forests and high carbon stock areas.
  • RSPO Certification: All our mills are certified by the Roundtable on Sustainable Palm Oil (RSPO).
  • Community Development: Supporting local communities through education, healthcare, and livelihood programs.
  • Biodiversity Conservation: Protecting wildlife corridors and endangered species habitats within our concessions.
  • Climate Action: Reducing greenhouse gas emissions through renewable energy and improved agricultural practices.
  • Supply Chain Transparency: Full traceability from plantation to end product with digital monitoring systems.
